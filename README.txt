A must have plugin that adds a YouTube subscribe button to your blog so people can subscribe to your YouTube channel without leaving your site.

== Description ==
= Pyts - =
Adds the YouTube Subscribe Button to your website.

It adds a widget to your blog that will display the YouTube Subscribe Button. The button lets people subscribe to your YouTube channel without having to leave your site to either log in to YouTube or confirm their subscriptions.

== Installation ==

1. Download youtube-subscribe-button.zip and unzip
2. Upload the unzipped pyts folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
3. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
4. Go to the 'Plugins' menu in Wordpress and add the widget to your sidebar.
5. Go to 'Appearence'->'Widgets' and in the "Youtube Subscribe Button" drop down form, input your YouTube channel id or name.
